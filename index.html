My First Web Visualization

This is an interactive visual of the Life Expectancy given the GDP per capita of the continents in the world in 2007. I created it using this tutorial.

The chart shows that life expectancy is an increasing function of GDP per capita. The colors of the circles represent the continents while the size is the population. GDP per capita is one of the many measures to judge the well-being of an economy and is the total income of a country normalized by its population, while the life expectancy is measured in number of years a person lives in a country.
